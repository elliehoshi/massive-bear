Peek

Web App

Description

Speed read NPR stories through Peek. Built using the NPR API, Peek aims to be the next evolution for delivering news content to anyone and everyone. Double-click an article to add to a list of stories you want to read that shows up in the column to the right. When you hover your mouse over the story on the right, click “Read”, and then it will bring you to a page where you can “Play” the story and it will play the words on the screen based on the Words Per Minute setting.

Tools Used

  • MongoDB
  • Express.js
  • Angular.js
  • Node.js
  • JavaScript
  • jQuery
